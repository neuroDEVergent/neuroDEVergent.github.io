I'm Petar, a graphics programmer and an indie game developer.

My main interests are real-time rendering, graphics APIs (OpenGL/Vulkan), with some indie game development on the side. I work primarily on Linux (Arch btw) and enjoy writing low level code, mostly in C and C++.

I have previous professional experience in IT, specifically in monitoring, network engineering as well as system and database administration, which got me used to working with and debugging complex infrastructure systems.

I actively study mathematics. Most of my knowledge is in the branch of linear algebra, but it extends into geometry and calculus. As well as in discrete mathematics, but I believe that goes without saying. :)

In addition to that, I like to teach basics of game development to beginners! From basic funcionalities of game engines, over programming concepts, all the way to the math needed.

Outside of programming, I play electric guitar to recharge and have an excuse to step away from the screen and put in hours of grunt work into something else, while keeping my curiosity and desire for deep, technical and theoretical understanding satisfied.
